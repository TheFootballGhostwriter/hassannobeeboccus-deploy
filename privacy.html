Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026

This privacy policy explains how Hassan Nobeeboccus ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use this website (hassannobeeboccus.com) and subscribe to email communications.

1. Data controller

Hassan Nobeeboccus is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. You can contact me at: thefootballghostwriter@outlook.com

2. What data I collect

I collect the following personal data:

  • Email address and first name when you subscribe to the email course or newsletter via the opt-in form on this site
  • Name, email and message content when you use the contact form
  • Usage data anonymised analytics data including pages visited, approximate geographic location (country level), device type and referral source

I do not collect payment information, sensitive personal data or data from children under 16.

3. How I collect your data

  • Email opt-in forms powered by MailerLite. When you submit the form, your data is processed by MailerLite on my behalf.
  • Contact form powered by Formspree. When you submit the contact form, your data is processed by Formspree and forwarded to me via email.
  • Analytics this site may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use the site. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

4. How I use your data

I use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the email course you signed up for
  • To respond to messages sent via the contact form
  • To send occasional updates about content, services or resources relevant to football development professionals (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To understand how visitors use this website so I can improve it

5. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

  • Consent when you submit the opt-in form, you consent to receiving the email course and related communications. You can withdraw this consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • Legitimate interest for anonymised website analytics, to improve the website and understand how it is used.

6. Third-party processors

Your data may be processed by the following third parties, all of which have their own privacy policies and data processing agreements:

  • MailerLite email service provider, used to store subscriber data and send emails (including both email courses). MailerLite is based in the European Union and complies with applicable data protection standards.
  • Formspree form processing service, used to handle contact form submissions.
  • Vercel website hosting provider.
  • Google Analytics web analytics service (loaded only with your cookie consent). IP addresses are anonymised before storage.

7. Data retention

I retain your email address and first name for as long as you remain subscribed to my email list. If you unsubscribe, your data will be removed from active mailing lists. MailerLite may retain a record of your email address to honour your unsubscribe request.

8. Your rights

Under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access the personal data I hold about you
  • The right to rectification if your data is inaccurate
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email me at thefootballghostwriter@outlook.com. I will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

This website uses essential cookies required for the site to function and, only with your explicit consent via the cookie banner, analytics cookies from Vercel Analytics and Google Analytics. Analytics cookies help me understand how visitors use the site so I can improve it. No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies are used. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via the cookie preferences button at the bottom of any page.

10. Changes to this policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, contact me at: thefootballghostwriter@outlook.com
